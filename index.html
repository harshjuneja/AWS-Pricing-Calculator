2. Estimate Costs for S3, EBS, EFS (Approx. US East - N. Virginia)

Understand tiered pricing models, select storage classes, and configure data transfer and replication for various storage services.

Amazon S3 (Object Storage)

  • 100 GB for static website, infrequent access: Approx. $2.30/month (S3 Standard-IA).
  • 500 GB, video lectures & documents, mixed access patterns: Approx. $11.50/month (S3 Standard-IA).
  • 5 TB, HD video hosting, frequently accessed globally: Approx. $0.023/GB for first 50TB (S3 Standard), so ~ $115/month.

Amazon EBS (Block Storage)

  • 1 General Purpose SSD (gp3) volume, 50 GB: Approx. $4.00/month ($0.08/GB-month).
  • 2 gp3 volumes (100 GB each) for app + DB: Approx. $16.00/month ($0.08/GB-month * 200 GB).
  • 4 io2 volumes (500 GB each) for high IOPS DB and critical apps: Approx. $250/month ($0.125/GB-month * 2000 GB).

Amazon EFS (File Storage)

  • 100 GB, burst mode, used by one dev server: Approx. $30.00/month ($0.30/GB-month Standard).
  • 500 GB shared by multiple dev/test EC2s: Approx. $150.00/month ($0.30/GB-month Standard).
  • 2 TB shared across analytics workloads, 10+ EC2s: Approx. $600.00/month ($0.30/GB-month Standard).
